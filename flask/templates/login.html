About K.J Somaiya College of Engineering

The K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE) was established in 1983 at Somaiya Vidyavihar, and affiliated with the University of Mumbai. It offers 4 year bachelors degree courses in the departments of Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering. KJSCE is an Autonomous college affiliated to the University of Mumbai spread across approx. 65 acres of posh land and stands among the top 3 engineering colleges in Mumbai.

About this project

This website will allow students to easily submit forms like internship form, proctor form, leave application, etc.
The proctor will recieve the form once you press submit.

The proctor can view student information and all the forms submitted by the student.
